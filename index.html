Data credits

Day texture: NASA Blue Marble Next Generation via NASA Global Imagery Browse Services (GIBS). Night texture: NASA Black Marble / VIIRS Black Marble via NASA Worldview and GIBS. This app fetches the imagery from NASA’s public GIBS WMS endpoint when the page loads.

Blue Marble and Black Marble are satellite-derived Earth imagery products. They are used here for educational visualisation.
